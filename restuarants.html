in Charlotte, North Carolina

Below I will list out my Favorite 5 Restuarants

Fav 5

  1. Bang Bang Burgers
    • Named Charlotte's best burger
    • Located in historic southend
    • Unique sides and toppings

  2. Queen City BBQ
    • Big menu and servings
    • Love the uptown location, right on 7th street
    • Game and event nights

  3. Iron Dish
    • All you can eat korean bbq
    • Located near UNC Charlotte
    • Cook it on your own right at your table

  4. Saigon Bay
    • Great pho at good prices
    • Located on the boardwalk

  5. Meng's Kitchen
    • Sometimes the best meal is the one you make at home
    • Located at my place
    • Show up anytime!
